About PolitiFoot

PolitiFoot is an attempt to objectively (i.e., non-normatively) categorize European football clubs based on the political ideology of their fanbases, specifically, major supporter groups. While many supporter groups are directly engaged in politics, the modern purchasing of clubs to be run as businesses has led to many club supporter groups no longer affiliating with a specific ideology. Because of this, PolitiFoot differentiates between clubs with fanbases that are currently politically affiliated and those that have been historically politically affiliated.

The database underpinning this project should not be seen as complete, but rather it is a living database, changing and being updated with new information. As such, we encourage users to make suggestions. If you think a club has been erroneously categorized, please click the suggestion button, and explain what you believe to be erroneous.

Note: Suggestions should be accompanied by citations linking to reputable sources (e.g., well-known newspapers, magazines and media outlets; academic researchers, etc.)
