About PolitiFoot

PolitiFoot is an attempt to objectively (i.e., non-normatively) categorize European football clubs based on the political ideology of their fanbases, specifically, major supporter groups.

The database underpinning this project should not be seen as complete, but rather it is a living database, changing and being updated with new information. As such, we encourage users to make suggestions. If you think a club has been erroneously categorized, please click the suggestion button, and explain what you believe to be erroneous.

Note: Suggestions should be accompanies by citations linking to reputably sources (e.g., well-known newspapers, magazines and media outlets; academic researchers, etc.)

Suggest a correction

Think a club is miscategorized, or missing a supporter group entirely? Let us know. Please include a source for any claim — a newspaper, magazine, established media outlet, or academic research — so it can be verified.
